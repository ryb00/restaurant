There are different restaurants, but "Foodie" is one. This is the name of our restaurant, which has long become a meeting place for connoisseurs of gourmet dishes and lovers of service. Exquisite service and delicious dishes from skilled chefs await you. Our menu has dishes for every taste. Our establishment is a great option to have a good time without spending a lot of money. Take a look at the menu and you'll see that food, drinks and a little dancing are affordable fun!
The spacious hall of the restaurant can accommodate 100 people. For accommodation - a choice of 2 to 10 people with soft sofas and armchairs. There are also special designated areas. The interior is a classic mix of coffee and golden shades. Romantic atmosphere, conditions for a decent holiday in pleasant company.
The menu offers a full range of healthy and tasty cuisine, from classic dishes to signature dishes (from the chef of the establishment). Expanded wine list with an emphasis on small producers. Large selection of wines. Whiskey and jeans from different countries. The author's take on classic cocktails.
Our restaurant menu offers dishes for vegans, vegetarians and people with allergies. There is a “dish of the day” on the menu every day. In addition, the restaurant regularly introduces new unique items to the menu.
Visit our restaurant, enjoy our sophisticated atmosphere and experience the excellent quality of food and service!
